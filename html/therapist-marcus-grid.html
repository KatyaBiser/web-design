Prof. Marcus Grid

CSS Grid Layout Architecture Expert

Biography

Professor Marcus Grid has dedicated his entire career to one noble mission: rescuing developers from the dark ages of `float: left` and endless Bootstrap columns. After witnessing too many talented coders suffer PTSD from clearfix hacks, he founded the Grid Recovery Institute in 2017.

"I've seen developers cry when they discover `grid-template-areas`," he often says with a knowing smile. His revolutionary approach combines cognitive behavioral therapy with hands-on grid exercises, helping patients gradually reduce their dependency on `position: absolute` as a coping mechanism.

Before joining Rehab from Vibe Coding, Prof. Grid spent years as a layout monk at Mozilla, where he meditated daily on the Zen of CSS Grid specification. He's known for his catchphrase: "Your divs don't need to float anymore. Let them be free on the grid."

Treatment Approach

Prof. Grid believes that every developer deserves to experience the joy of `display: grid` without shame or judgment. His gentle, evidence-based methodology includes:

  • Safe space for admitting you still use floats
  • Guided grid-template-columns breathing exercises
  • Support groups for former Bootstrap users
  • One-on-one grid debugging sessions with compassion
  • Weekly "Grid Wins" celebration meetings
  • Emergency hotline for clearfix relapses
